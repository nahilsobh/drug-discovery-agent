# RedClaw AI Factory — Recommended Agent Questions

Questions designed for new drug design and strategic discovery using ClinicalTrials.gov, Open Targets, and Europe PMC.

---

## Target Identification

```
"Which gene targets have high Open Targets scores in [disease] that no pharma company is currently drugging?"
"What targets are shared between Alzheimer's and Parkinson's with bio score > 0.7?"
"Which gene targets in autoimmune disease have genetic evidence but no approved drug?"
"Find targets with strong somatic mutation evidence in pancreatic cancer where RedClaw has no trial"
```

---

## Indication Expansion

```
"What other diseases could Giredestrant's ESR1 target address beyond breast cancer?"
"Find all RedClaw assets where the primary target has strong evidence in a rare disease with no approved therapy"
"Which RedClaw drugs have a bio score above 0.65 in an indication they are not currently being trialled for?"
"What new indications does Open Targets suggest for Trontinemab's PTPN6 target?"
```

## Combination Strategy

